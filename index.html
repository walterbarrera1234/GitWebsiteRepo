After a SKU is cleanly shipped, we can often observe a natural decay in sales velocity over time. This post discusses a method to estimate the decay curve using shipment date and sell-through data.

Simple Decay Model

Assuming exponential decay, we can write:

\[ S(t) = S_0 \cdot e^{-kt} \]

Where \( S_0 \) is initial sales rate and \( k \) is the decay rate. The goal is to estimate \( k \) for various SKUs.
